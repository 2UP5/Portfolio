About

Prestige Blooming is a visionary business dedicated to transforming the gardens of homes into accessible retreats and havens of natural beauty and tranquility. We specialise in helping individuals bring their garden ideas to life by seamlessly integrating their ideas with their existing living spaces. Our mission is to create affordables oases of relaxation and serenity for families, offering a respite from the demands of their daily lives.

Originally from Jamaica, the owner, Shirley Bailey has lived in the UK since 2002. From a farming family where both parents are farmers, Shirley grew up in the countryside surrounded by temperate and tropical plants alike. Part of this is the reason why her love for plants has grown exponentially. While she has studied various subjects and taken different career paths, her love for the outdoors was always there from her upbringing. Growing both indoor and outdoor plants, she now owns an allotment where she extends her love for the outdoors.

Benefits for customers:

  • Affordable and customised garden design.
  • Increased property value.
  • A relaxing retreat for families.
  • Improved well-being and mental health through natural surroundings.
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly garden solutions.
