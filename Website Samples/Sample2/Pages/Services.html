Services

Our services offer affordable garden design solutions that beautify homes while promoting family well-being. We prioritise cost effectiveness, creativity, sustainability, family-oriented spaces and a personalised approach to ensure that every garden we create is a unique reflection of our clients' desires with peace of mind to clients who wish to transform their gardens without breaking the bank. Besides just transforming your space, property value will increase and well-being and mental health will improve as experience and studies into the relation between natural surroundings and the human mind.

Range of services available:

  • Garden Integration: Specialisation in seamlessly integrating gardens with existing home spaces, ensuring a harmonious transition from indoor to outdoor living therefore being cost-effective and sustainable.
  • Seasonal Planting: Seasonal vegetation both indoors and outdoors
  • Garden cleanups
  • Organic Plants: Personally grown plants and vegetables for sale (English and Jamaican vegetables)
  • Tropical Planting

Variety of bookings available weekly, fortnightly or monthly.
